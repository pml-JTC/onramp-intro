Learning to Code Will Open Doors to a Career in Mental Healthcare

This site gives an overview of the different ways I might use code to improve mental healthcare

Mental health awareness is higher than ever, yet mental healthcare stands to improve in many ways

Since losing my mother to suicide I have wanted to contribute to the improvement of mental healthcare. A lot is the same as it was 30 years ago:

  • Mental illnesses remain poorly understood
  • Pill burden is still often high for people managing severe conditions
  • Stigma, while decreasing, is still a main barrier that prevents people from seeking help

Many areas of research are interesting to me, but my overall goal is to protect lives through increasing knowledge

A lot of the issues with mental healthcare stem from lacking an understanding of disease etiology. I am most interested in:

  • How valuation and decision-making are coupled with stress management
  • How social determinants of health influence the incidence rates and outcomes of mental health challenges
  • How non-pharmacological interventions, including brain-computer interface technology might make treatment more precise

Several career options align with these unmet needs and my interests

Coding is needed in all of the following, but might be applied in different ways:

  • Computational Psychiatry Research: experimental design, data acquisition and analysis, modeling
  • Public Mental Health: predictive analytics, machine learning, data analysis for policy reform
  • Neurotechnology: processing vast amounts of data, creating simulations, building device application software

Key coding languages that will improve mental healthcare

While at the very beginning of my coding journey, I hope to eventually be able to work with:

  1. Python
  2. R and MATLAB
  3. Java
  4. C/C++
  5. Julia

Coding applied to mental healthcare will save lives

I'm excited to see where exactly my coding journey will lead, but when applied to research, public health, and technology, coding will save lives!
